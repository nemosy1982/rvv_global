DNA Sequencing machine

A DNA sequencing machine is a specialized laboratory instrument that reads the precise order of nucleotides (A, T, C, G) in a DNA molecule. It uses techniques like fluorescence detection or nanopore sensing to generate digital data that represents the genetic code of an organism..

Ventilators

Ventilators are medical devices that support or take over the breathing process for patients who are unable to breathe adequately on their own. They deliver a controlled flow of oxygen-rich air into the lungs and remove carbon dioxide, using adjustable settings for pressure, volume, and rate. Ventilators are essential in critical care settings, especially for patients with respiratory failure, during surgery, or in severe cases of illness like pneumonia or COVID-19.

$10,000

PRF Bio Centiruge

PRF (Platelet Rich Fibrin) Bio Centrifuge is a specialized laboratory device used to separate blood components, particularly for preparing platelet-rich fibrin from whole blood. This centrifuge operates at specific speeds and durations to concentrate platelets and growth factors, which are then used in regenerative medicine, dental procedures, and wound healing to promote tissue repair and healing.

$499

Chemical Lab Equipment

Chemical lab equipment refers to the various tools and instruments used in laboratories for conducting experiments, analyzing substances, and performing chemical reactions. This includes beakers, flasks, pipettes, burettes, test tubes, and analytical balances. Each piece of equipment serves a specific purpose, such as measuring liquids, mixing chemicals, or heating substances, and is essential for ensuring accuracy and safety in chemical research and analysis.

$499

Microscope

Microscopes are instruments used to see objects that are too small for the naked eye. They are essential tools in laboratories, allowing scientists to observe the fine details of biological samples, materials, and other specimens. Microscopes can be optical, using lenses to magnify images, or electron-based, providing higher resolution by using beams of electrons. They play a crucial role in fields such as biology, materials science, and medicine.

$499

Clinical Chemistry Analyzer

Clinical chemistry analyzer is a medical laboratory device used to measure the concentration of chemical substances in blood, urine, or other body fluids. It automates testing for a wide range of analytes such as glucose, enzymes, electrolytes, and lipids to help diagnose and monitor diseases. These analyzers use various detection methods like photometry, ion-selective electrodes, or turbidimetry for accurate and rapid results.
