Choosing the right size of a solar power system is the first and most important step in your solar journey. At RV&V Global, we help you calculate this based on your daily electricity usage and the appliances you depend on.

3. Consider Energy Efficiency

Energy-efficient LED bulbs, inverter refrigerators, and newer appliances help you get more done with less power.

4. Factor in Solar Battery Backup

If you want 24/7 power, you need enough battery storage to cover nighttime use. A solar expert can help match your usage to the right battery size.

5. Account for Future Growth

Think ahead. Will you buy a freezer or add more ACs soon? Plan a slightly higher capacity to accommodate growth without stress.
