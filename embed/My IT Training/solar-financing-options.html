At RV&V Global Solar Resources, we understand that the initial cost of going solar can be a concern. That's why we offer various financing options to make your solar investment more accessible. Whether you're a homeowner, business owner, or a community leader, you can benefit from our flexible plans.

1. Pay Small Small (PSS) Plan

Our Pay Small Small (PSS) plan allows you to install your solar system and pay in manageable monthly installments. This plan makes solar more affordable and allows you to begin saving on your electricity bills immediately.

2. Solar Loans

If you're interested in a loan option, we partner with trusted financial institutions that offer competitive interest rates for solar installations. These loans are designed to be paid back in manageable terms, with low monthly payments.

3. Government Incentives & Grants

In 2025, several Nigerian states and energy bodies are offering incentives for businesses and homes that choose solar. These grants and rebates can significantly reduce your initial investment, making solar energy more affordable.

4. Corporate Financing Solutions

For businesses, we provide corporate financing solutions that allow for long-term repayment options. This includes interest-free payment plans and flexible terms to ensure your company can benefit from solar without straining its budget.

5. Solar Subscription Model

With the solar subscription model, you pay a fixed monthly fee for solar power usage without owning the system. This is ideal for those who want the benefits of solar energy without the commitment of ownership.
