Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 23, 2025

RV&V Global Resources ("RV&V", "we", "us" or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain and protect personal information when you visit our websites, use our services, interact with our apps, apply for jobs, attend our training, or otherwise engage with RV&V Global Resources.

1. Scope & Acceptance

This policy applies to personal data collected through our websites (including subdomains and microsites), mobile applications, events, training and recruitment processes, and through other interactions with RV&V Global Resources or our authorized partners. By accessing or using our services you acknowledge that you accept this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Personal Information (Identifiable)

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone numbers, postal address.
  • Professional details: job title, employer, CV / resume, education, qualifications.
  • Account credentials: username, password (hashed), profile information.
  • Financial information: billing address, tax information, bank details or payment card details when you make purchases or enroll in paid programs (processed by our payment providers).
  • Communications: messages you send us via forms, email, live chat or social channels.
  • Photographs and media: images or video you provide in connection with events, case studies or training.

2.2 Non-Personal & Technical Information

  • Device & browser data (platform, browser type/version).
  • Usage data: pages viewed, time spent on pages, referral source, clicks and other interaction logs.
  • IP address, approximate geolocation, and network provider.
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies section below).

2.3 Special Categories of Data

We generally do not collect sensitive personal data (special category data) such as health, race, political opinion, religion or biometrics. If you provide such data voluntarily—for example for a scholarship application or a medical condition required for participation— we will only process it where we have a lawful basis and with your explicit consent, and we will protect it with additional safeguards.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you contact us, register, apply, subscribe, purchase, attend training or events, or use our services.
  • Automatically via cookies, web beacons, server logs and analytics when you visit our websites or use our apps.
  • From third parties (payment providers, recruitment platforms, public sources, partners and service providers) when you interact through those channels.

4. Purposes & Legal Bases for Processing

We use personal data for the following purposes (and where applicable, the legal basis):

  • Provision of services: to deliver and manage training, consultancy, installations and products (Contract performance).
  • Customer support & communication: to respond to inquiries, provide support and send transactional information (Contract performance / Legitimate interest).
  • Billing & payments: to issue invoices and process payments (Contract performance / Legal obligations).
  • Recruitment & HR: to evaluate and manage job applications (Legitimate interest / Consent where required).
  • Marketing & newsletters: to send promotional materials and news (Consent where required; Legitimate interest with opt-out in some jurisdictions).
  • Analytics & site improvement: to analyze usage patterns and improve our services (Legitimate interest).
  • Legal & compliance: to comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights (Legal obligation / Legitimate interest).

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage and similar technologies to operate our website, understand usage, and personalise content. Cookies fall into categories:

  • Strictly necessary: required for basic site functions (login, forms). These cannot be switched off.
  • Performance & analytics: to measure visits and traffic sources (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional: to remember preferences, region, or language choices.
  • Advertising & targeting: to deliver relevant marketing and measure campaigns.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and, where available, our cookie consent tool. Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We may share personal information with the following categories of recipients:

  • Service providers: companies that provide payment processing, hosting, analytics, email delivery, CRM and other business services.
  • Professional advisors: lawyers, auditors or consultants to support our business operations and compliance.
  • Partners & collaborators: for jointly delivered projects, training or events (only where necessary and subject to contractual safeguards).
  • Legal authorities: where required by law, regulation or court order, or to defend our legal rights.
  • Acquisitions & corporate transactions: in the event of a sale, merger, re-organisation or financing, user data may be transferred as part of the transaction (with notice to users where required).

We do not sell personal data for direct money consideration. If this changes, we will update this policy and provide opt-out mechanisms as required by law.

7. Third-Party Services & Links

Our website and services may include links to third-party sites, services and widgets (e.g., payment gateways, social networks, video hosts). These third parties have their own privacy practices. We are not responsible for their content or practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

8. International Transfers

RV&V is an international business. Personal data may be transferred, stored or processed in countries outside your jurisdiction (including countries without the same level of data protection). Where transfers are necessary, we will implement appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses, data processing agreements, binding corporate rules or other lawful mechanisms).

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this policy, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Specific retention periods depend on the nature of the data (e.g., financial records, recruitment data, user accounts). If you want details on retention periods for a specific category of data, contact the Data Protection Officer (see Contact section).

10. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. Measures include encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, data minimisation, staff training, and periodic security testing. While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure — if a breach occurs, we will follow applicable notification laws and inform affected individuals and regulators as required.

11. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental or guardian consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal data of a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

12. Your Rights & Choices

The rights listed below depend on jurisdiction and may require identity verification. Where rights exist under applicable law, you may:

  • Access: request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: request deletion of personal data where there is no legal reason to retain it (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: request restriction of processing for certain purposes.
  • Object: object to processing based on legitimate interests, including profiling for direct marketing.
  • Data portability: request transfer of your data to another provider in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw consent: when processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time (this will not affect processing already carried out lawfully).

To exercise rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at the details below. We will respond within the timeline required by applicable law (where one exists).

13. Specific Notices for EU / EEA (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the lawful bases for our processing are set out above (contract, legal obligation, consent, legitimate interests). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection regulator. We process personal data in accordance with the GDPR where applicable.

14. Specific Notices for Residents of California (CCPA / CPRA-style)

California residents may have additional rights regarding the personal information we collect, including the right to request disclosures about categories of personal information collected, sold, or disclosed for a business purpose, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (if any). RV&V does not knowingly "sell" personal information as defined under CCPA without providing consumer choice. For requests under California law, please contact us using the contact details below and state "California privacy request".

15. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

We generally do not make decisions based solely on automated processing that have legal or similarly significant effects. If we do use automated profiling or make automated decisions (for example in candidate screening or targeted marketing), you will be informed, and we will provide information about the logic involved and your rights to object or request human review where required by law.

16. Marketing & Communications

We may contact you with newsletters, event invitations, and marketing about our services. You can opt-out at any time through unsubscribe links in emails or by contacting us directly. We may send transactional messages (service updates, confirmations) which are necessary for the service and are not marketing.
